Testimony

"See3D has been a wonderful and valuable resource for my students who are blind and visually impaired. They absolutely love the tactual models of their favorite movie characters such as R2D2 from Star Wars and the Minions. My personal favorites are the butterflies and snowflakes. It helps them to understand the intricate details in the many things we encounter. I cannot thank See3D enough for the amazing tactile models they have provided to my students to help them “see” the world!"

-Michelle Quinn
